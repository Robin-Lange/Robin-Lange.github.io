About me.

Mechanical Engineering student from Bavaria, Germany, studying at Georgia Southern University with a Vehicle Design and Testing emphasis. President's List every semester since enrollment. Allen E. Paulson Scholar. Honors College.

I research at the Intelligent Vehicle Laboratory, building ROS2-based perception and control pipelines for autonomous platforms using LiDAR, 4D-Radar, and RGB-D cameras. Published at SAE World Congress and presented at multiple regional and honors conferences.

Interned at Fraunhofer IVI, CARISSMA C-ISAFE, Airbus Defence and Space, and Airbus Helicopters.

Languages: German (native), English (fluent), French (intermediate).
